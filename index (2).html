About WordCounter Pro

WordCounter Pro was built with a single mission: to provide the cleanest, most reliable word counting experience on the internet. Unlike other tools that bombard you with pop-ups, banner ads, and unnecessary distractions, we believe that productivity tools should stay out of your way.

Why We Built This

As writers, students, and digital marketers ourselves, we were frustrated by the state of existing word counters. Most were cluttered with advertisements, slow to load, and required account creation for basic features. We decided to build something better - a tool that respects your time, your privacy, and your workflow.

Key Features

  • Real-time Analysis: Instant word, character, sentence, and paragraph counting as you type
  • Reading Time Estimator: Calculate how long your content takes to read based on average adult reading speed (238 WPM)
  • Speaking Time Calculator: Perfect for speechwriters and presenters (130 WPM average)
  • Keyword Density Analysis: SEO-friendly word frequency tracking to optimize your content
  • 100% Privacy: All processing happens in your browser. Your text never leaves your device
  • Zero Ads: Completely free with no advertisements, pop-ups, or distractions
  • Export Options: Copy to clipboard or download as .txt file with one click

Who Uses WordCounter Pro?

Our users include bestselling authors drafting novels, university students writing dissertations, content marketers optimizing blog posts, SEO professionals analyzing competitor content, journalists meeting word count deadlines, and translators ensuring consistent text length across languages.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is WordCounter Pro really free?

Yes, absolutely. WordCounter Pro is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, no premium tiers, and no account requirements. We believe essential writing tools should be accessible to everyone.

Is my text stored or sent to a server?

No. All text processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your content is never transmitted to any server, stored in any database, or accessible to anyone else. You can even use WordCounter Pro offline after the initial page load.

How accurate is the reading time estimate?

Our reading time calculation uses the standard average adult reading speed of 238 words per minute (WPM) for silent reading, and 130 WPM for speaking aloud. These are research-backed averages from studies by Marc Brysbaert and others. For technical or dense content, actual reading time may be longer.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Yes, WordCounter Pro is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers. The interface automatically adapts to your screen size.

Can I count words in multiple languages?

Yes, WordCounter Pro works with any language that uses space-separated words, including English, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, and many others. For languages like Chinese, Japanese, and Korean that do not use spaces, character counting is fully accurate.

What file formats can I download?

Currently, you can download your text as a plain .txt file. We are considering adding support for .docx and .pdf exports in future updates based on user feedback.

How do I use the keyword density feature?

Simply type or paste your text into the editor. The keyword density section automatically updates to show the most frequently used words, excluding common stop words like "the," "and," and "is." This helps SEO professionals identify overused or underused terms.
